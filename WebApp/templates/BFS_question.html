CoronaVirus

Recently, a new virus broke out in k cities in a country at time t=0. Country can be viewed as a (n x m) grid((0, 0) at the top left and (n-1, m-1) at the bottom right). In every second, the virus spreads from the infected cities to its neighbours that is the cells directly connected to it(up, right, bottom,left). You have to print the time for every city when it got infected first.

Constraints

1≤n,m≤1000

1≤k;≤n x m

Input

First Line contains n, m, k the number of rows, columns in the grid and the number cities which got infected at t=0. The next k lines contains 2 integers i,j denoting the cell infected

Output

Print N lines, each line containing M space seperated integers indicating the first time at which i,j city got infected

Sample Input

 3 3 1
 1 1

Sample Output

 2 1 2
 1 0 1
 2 1 2
